Our Story

India's Manufacturing Strength, Delivered Globally

TERNS EXIM was founded with a single vision: to be the most reliable bridge between India's world-class fastener manufacturers and global industrial buyers. Based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu — the manufacturing heartland of India — we leverage decades of regional expertise to source, certify, and export premium fasteners to buyers across three continents.

As a registered merchant exporter, we go beyond just fasteners. We offer comprehensive export solutions that allow international buyers to consolidate multiple product needs under one trusted relationship — saving time, reducing cost, and ensuring consistent quality.
