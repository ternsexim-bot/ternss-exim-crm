TERNS EXIM exports a full range of anchor bolts — including J-bolts, L-bolts, U-bolts, and eye bolts — from Coimbatore, India to construction and infrastructure buyers across the Middle East, USA, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Our anchor bolts are manufactured to ASTM F1554 (Grades 36, 55, 105) and IS 1367, and are widely used to anchor structural steel columns, equipment bases, transmission towers, and industrial machinery to concrete foundations.

All anchor bolts are available in carbon steel, stainless steel 304/316, and alloy steel in diameters M6 to M100 with custom lengths up to 3000 mm. We supply with full Mill Test Certificates, and third-party inspection by SGS or Bureau Veritas is available upon request for project-critical orders.

Product Variants

Types of Anchor Bolts We Export

Pre-formed anchor bolt shapes for cast-in-place and post-installed anchoring in concrete and masonry.

J-Bolt

J-shaped for cast-in-place anchoring. Standard choice for machinery bases and structural columns.

L-Bolt

L-shaped for cast-in-concrete anchoring where horizontal pull-out resistance is required.

U-Bolt

U-shaped for clamping pipes, rods, and tubes to structural members. Available with nut and plate sets.

Eye Bolt

Closed or open eye loop for lifting, rigging, and cable anchoring applications.

Rag Bolt

Splayed base for superior pull-out resistance in stone and brick masonry construction.

Foundation Stud

Double-ended anchor rod with threaded both ends, used in heavy machinery seating plates.
