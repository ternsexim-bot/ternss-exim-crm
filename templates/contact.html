Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order: 10,000 pieces or 700 kg depending on product. For new buyers, we welcome trial orders. Contact us with your requirement and we'll advise on the best option.

What countries do you ship to?

We ship worldwide — with strong presence in USA, UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, Germany, Netherlands, Australia, and throughout Southeast Asia. Shipping is handled from Chennai and Tuticorin ports.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept T/T (Bank Transfer), Letter of Credit (L/C), and Western Union. Standard terms: 30-50% advance, balance against Bill of Lading or L/C at sight.

How long does delivery take?

Standard stock items: 2–4 weeks from order confirmation. Custom or bulk orders: 4–8 weeks. We provide estimated delivery dates at the time of order confirmation.

Do you provide quality test certificates?

Yes. Mill Test Certificates (MTC), Chemical & Mechanical Test Reports, and third-party inspection (SGS, BV, etc.) are available on request.

Can you export non-fastener products?

Yes — as a Merchant Exporter, we can source and export a wide range of Indian-manufactured goods. Use the Merchant Export Inquiry form to tell us what you need.
