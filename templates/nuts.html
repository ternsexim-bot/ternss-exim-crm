Product Variants

Types of Nuts We Export

Full range of nut types for every application — from standard structural hex nuts to specialty locknuts and coupling nuts.

Hex Nut

DIN 934 / ISO 4032. Standard six-sided nut for general fastening in all structural and mechanical applications.

Heavy Hex Nut

ASTM A563 / DIN 6915. Larger across-flats for bolted flanges, structural steel, and high-strength bolt assemblies.

Nyloc Lock Nut

DIN 985. Nylon insert prevents loosening from vibration. Ideal for dynamic and high-vibration machinery applications.

Flange Nut

DIN 6923. Integrated serrated flange acts as a built-in washer, distributing load and resisting rotation.

Castle (Slotted) Nut

DIN 935. Slots engage with cotter pin to prevent rotation. Used on spindles, steering, and safety-critical joints.

Coupling (Hexagon Long) Nut

DIN 6334. Extended body for joining two threaded rods end-to-end. Used in anchor rod installations and extensions.
