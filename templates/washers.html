Product Variants

Types of Washers We Export

Complete washer range — from standard flat and spring types to high-load Belleville disc springs and oversized fender washers.

Flat Washer

DIN 125 / ISO 7089. Standard plain washer for distributing bolt load and preventing surface damage.

Spring Washer

DIN 127. Split ring design creates spring tension to resist loosening under vibration and dynamic loading.

Lock Washer

DIN 6798. Serrated internal or external teeth bite into the mating surface to prevent nut rotation under vibration.

Belleville Disc Spring

DIN 2093. Conical disc for high-load spring applications, preload maintenance, and thermal expansion compensation.

Fender / Dock Washer

Oversized OD for thin or soft materials. Provides large bearing area to prevent pull-through in timber, GRP, and sheet metal.

Structural / Hardened Washer

ASTM F436. F436 hardened washer for use with structural bolts (ASTM A325 / A490) in steel construction.
